Why I Love Reading

Reading has always been my escape. Whether it's a cozy rainy afternoon or a late night when I can't sleep, a good book has always been there for me. I love how stories can transport you to entirely different worlds, introduce you to characters that feel like old friends, and teach you something new about life without you even realizing it.

My favorite genres are romantasy, literary fiction, and the occasional self-improvement book when I'm feeling motivated. I'm always on the lookout for my next favorite read!

My Reading Corner Aesthetic

There's something magical about a cozy reading setup — a warm drink, good lighting, and a book you can't put down.
